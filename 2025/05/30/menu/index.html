Yuxin Yang

I am currently a graduate student supervised by Fang Wang in Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics, affiliated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I obtained my bachelor degree from the School of Computer Science at HUST. My research interest lies in database systems(especially in index structures) and distributed file systems. Feel free to email me if you want to contact me.
